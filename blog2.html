Weekend budget trip to Udaipur from Delhi !

  • Udaipur is famous for beautiful lakes, forts, royal palaces, and museums which depicts the rich cultural heritage of the state. It is in the southern part of Rajasthan and is surrounded by the Aravali Range.


    Cheapest way to Reach Udaipur.


    The city is very well connected with major Indian cities .One of the cheapest way to reach Udaipur is by train .You can get a Sleeper Class train under 500rsüòØ. Board a train from Hazrat Nizamuddin Railway Station around 6pm on Thursday .

    Day :1


    You will reach Udaipur City Railway Station on next morning i.e. Friday around 9am. From Udaipur Railway Station reach Gangaur Ghat (which is somewhere 3km from station). Gangaur Ghat is situated near Lake Pichola .

    After reaching Gangaur Ghat book a dorms(if travelling alone) or a budget room(if you are two people), you can easily get a room in approx. 500-600rs (bargaining is must üòù)

    After booking room cover all the near places by walk. places one should cover in Day 1 are Gangaur Ghat , Jagdish Temple,City Palace and Lake Pichola View Point. In evening book a scooty(350-400rs/day Rental max.) for next day and enjoy night-view from a must visit ghat of Udaipur known as Ambrai ghat (Timing 8-8).

    I am sure you will fall in love with the night-view from Ambrai Ghat.

    Day :2


    Wakeup early morning around 5-6am and head directly to Badi-Lake, spend a good 3/4hours there .Don't forget to click awesome scenic photographs there for flaunting on Instagram. This is place is soo peaceful and scenic , one of the off-beat location in Udaipur and I am assuring you that you won't regret spending time there. Now from Badi-Lake ride towards Sajangarh-Fort also known as Monsoon-Palace . From Monsoon-Palace headback to your room .Have a bath ,Eat something ,Refresh yourself and get ready for evening boat-ride in Fatehsagar Lake(If you are on tight budget) otherwise in Lake-Picola.

    Day :3


    On Day 3 you will be covering places like Karni-Mata Temple (via Rope-way). After that you will be directly headed towards Saash-Bahu Temple (some 25km away from your city) roads are pretty awesome ,you will definitely enjoy roada towards Saash-Bahu Temple. Architecture of temple is pretty goood , a place worth visit.

    After returning back : board your train from Udaipur-city at around 6pm. You will reach Delhi next day by 7am .
